We're currently making Lasertag

Lasertag is a game for Meta Quest 3 and 3S that turns any space into a laser tag arena for you and your friends

No setup required

Lasertag scans your environment while you play, and players connect automatically with a single button press, so jumping in is super easy. Grab your friends, find a space, and cause chaos.
